About the Book

Music Licensing Made Simple" by Aditya Jagdhane is a clear, beginner-friendly guide for musicians, singers, and content creators. It explains why cover songs can’t simply be uploaded to streaming platforms, what copyright means, and how digital and mechanical licenses work. Written in plain English, it breaks down complex music licensing concepts so you can legally release covers, avoid copyright issues, and manage royalties correctly. Ideal for independent artists navigating today’s music industry.

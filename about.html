Author Background

Aditya Jagdhane is an Indian musician, singer-songwriter, and composer known for blending heartfelt lyrics with soulful, reflective melodies. His work often explores emotion, identity, and the subtle moments that shape human experience. With a creative voice rooted in honesty and feeling, Aditya’s music has grown into a personal language of expression.

Beyond music, Aditya is a graphic designer, programmer, and philosopher with a deep interest in psychology and human behavior. His multidisciplinary background enables him to move fluidly between artistic and technical mediums, allowing each skill to influence the other. Whether designing visuals, crafting digital experiences, or composing music, he approaches every project with intention and introspection.

Aditya’s creative work frequently reflects themes of emotion, self-awareness, and the inner world. His artistic philosophy centers on exploring how creativity can help us understand ourselves and connect more deeply with others.

He is also the founder of IndiePulse Records, a creative space built to support independent artists and amplify authentic voices. Through this platform and his personal projects, he continues to experiment, learn, and build meaningful work across multiple disciplines.

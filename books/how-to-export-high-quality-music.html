About the Book

Master the art of exporting professional-grade music from FL Studio 20 with this practical guide. Learn how to create crystal-clear audio files that sound great on any platform—from streaming services to radio stations.

This eBook covers essential export settings, a step-by-step export process, and common mistakes to avoid.

Inside, you’ll learn:

  • Essential Export Settings: How sample rate, bit depth, file format, and headroom affect sound quality.
  • Step-by-Step Export Process: A clear six-step workflow from export menu to final render.
  • File Format Comparison: Key differences between WAV, MP3, and FLAC.
  • Avoiding Common Mistakes: Prevent clipping, low bitrates, and mismatched sample rates.
  • Pro Tips for Perfect Exports: Expert techniques for referencing, A/B testing, and consistent workflows.
